Having used WordPress for many years, I did a full redesign of my webpage in 2026. I decided to build a new site from scratch as an experiment, and ended up building a simple, PHP-based CMS that renders markdown-files - inspired by [Grav](https://getgrav.org) and similar systems. The source code is available on [Github](https://github.com/bardlahn/bard.lahn.no) in case anyone is interested to take a look.

Building my own site was also a way of making sure I do not rely on any third-party trackers or gather any form of personal data. The site does not use cookies. I use the open-source tool [GoatCounter](https://www.goatcounter.com) to gather basic statistics on site visits and use. I want to contribute as much as possible to building an open web and to promote the use of [open digital tools](https://github.com/bardlahn/academic-open) that do not increase the power and profit of big tech.
